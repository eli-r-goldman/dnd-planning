Once, there was a god known by many names—in the simplest of terms, it was the Ultimate God. It crafted the universe itself, and when It grew bored of Its very own cosmos, It created a world in which It could reside. This planet is called Astravaile.

Before the God could populate the planet, It needed to decide what sort of world this would be. It needed to decide what kind of inhabitants It could accept. This decision was the biggest of the God's life, and It could not decide. This choice caused It so much strife that It couldn't help but to shatter and break apart. The Ultimate God was no longer. Instead, every piece of It—Its organs, Its extremities, Its blood—was converted into newer and splintered Gods, each of which ruled over their own domain.

The Verity

The religion of Astravaile is known as The Verity, a pantheon made of real and true gods. The question is never if you believe, but rather who you devote yourself to.

The Phalangeoi

It is said that the ten fingers that crafted Astravaile became the Phalangeoi, the major players in The Verity's pantheon.
